Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does loan approval take?
Loan approval typically takes 24 hours. Once approved, funds are disbursed within the same day.
What documents do I need to apply?
You need a valid ID, BVN, bank statement (3 months), proof of address, and employment/business documents.
What is the interest rate?
Interest rates vary from 5% to 15% depending on loan type, amount, and duration. Contact us for specific rates.
Can I repay my loan early?
Yes, you can repay your loan early without penalties. Early repayment may reduce total interest paid.
What happens if I miss a payment?
Late payment fees apply. Contact us immediately if you anticipate payment difficulties to discuss options.
How do I get proof of funds?
Contact us with your requirements. We provide verified proof of funds documents within 24-48 hours.
Is my information secure?
Yes, we use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information.
Can I apply for multiple loans?
You can apply for a new loan after successfully repaying your current loan.
