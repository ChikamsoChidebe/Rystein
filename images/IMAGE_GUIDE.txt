===========================
1. Choose images with Nigerian/African representation when possible
2. Ensure images are high quality and professional
3. Maintain consistent style across all images
4. Optimize images for web before uploading (use tools like TinyPNG.com)
5. Use images that reflect your brand colors (yellow, black, white)
6. Ensure images are properly licensed for commercial use
